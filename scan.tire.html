Tread Depth Guidelines

New: 8-10mm
Good: 4-8mm
Replace: Below 2mm
Legal minimum: 1.6mm (most countries)

Warning Signs

• Cracks in sidewall
• Bulges or blisters
• Uneven wear patterns
• Excessive vibration
• Age over 6 years

Maintenance Tips

• Check pressure monthly
• Rotate every 8,000-10,000km
• Align wheels annually
• Avoid overloading
• Store properly in off-season

Safety First

Always consult a professional tire technician for safety-critical decisions. This tool provides guidance only and should not replace expert inspection.
