Shop Tool Summary — All Three Brands
🔧

Chevy — 15mm

Covers 95%+ of all Chevrolets. Keep 14mm for Prizm/Metro and 16mm backup for 2001–16 Duramax HD oil drain plug.

🔴

Toyota — 14mm

Covers every Toyota with a gas engine, 2000–2026. M12x1.25. Always replace crush washer. Torque ~27–33 ft-lbs.

🔵

Honda — 17mm

Covers every Honda with a gas engine, 2000–2026. M14x1.5. Always replace crush washer. Torque ~29 ft-lbs.

⚙️

Filter Wrench

Band wrench for spin-on. 32mm for Chevy cartridge (2014+ 1500, L5P). Cap wrench for Toyota/Honda cartridge-style filters on newer models.

📐

Torque Wrench

Chevy gas: ~18 ft-lbs · Duramax 01–16: ~44 ft-lbs · Toyota: ~27–33 ft-lbs · Honda: ~29 ft-lbs

🪣

Drain Pan

4–5 qt for small cars. 8 qt for most trucks/SUVs. 12 qt for Duramax HD (10 qt capacity).
