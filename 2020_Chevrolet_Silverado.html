2020 SILVERADO OIL LIFE RESET GUIDE

Model: 1500 LT | Engine: 5.3L V8

Reset Methods

Driver Information Center

Primary Method
Start The Ignition

Turn the ignition to the "ON" position without starting the engine. For push-button start vehicles, press the START button twice without pressing the brake pedal.

Access The DIC Menu

Use the steering wheel controls to navigate through the Driver Information Center (DIC) menu. Press the thumbwheel button or arrow buttons to cycle through the available options.

Find Oil Life Display

Navigate to the "Vehicle Information" menu, then scroll until you find "OIL LIFE REMAINING" or "REMAINING OIL LIFE" on the display.

Reset The Oil Life

Press and hold the thumbwheel button (or check mark button) on the steering wheel for approximately 5 seconds until the display flashes and confirms the reset.

Verify Reset

The oil life percentage should now display 100%. If it does not, repeat steps 3-4. Turn off the ignition to complete the process.

Pro Tip

Always reset your oil life monitor immediately after an oil change to ensure accurate maintenance tracking. This helps maintain your vehicle's warranty and ensures proper service intervals.

Accelerator Pedal Method

Alternative
Prepare The Vehicle

Sit in the driver's seat and close all doors. Turn the ignition to the "ON" position (or press the START button twice without pressing the brake) but DO NOT start the engine.

Locate The Accelerator

Make sure the vehicle is in PARK, then locate the accelerator pedal (gas pedal). Ensure nothing is blocking your access to fully depress the pedal.

Perform The Reset Sequence

Firmly press the accelerator pedal all the way to the floor and then fully release it. Repeat this action three times within five seconds.

Watch For Confirmation

On the third press, hold the pedal down for a moment. The "OIL LIFE RESET" or "CHANGE OIL SOON" message should flash on the display, indicating a successful reset.

Complete The Process

Release the accelerator pedal and turn off the ignition. When you restart the vehicle, the oil life indicator should now be reset to 100%.

Important Note

If this method doesn't work after several attempts, try the primary method using the Driver Information Center controls. Some newer Silverado models may have phased out this feature.

Control Locations

The thumbwheel button (marked with OK) is located on the right side of the steering wheel.

Oil Change Specifications

Recommended Oil

Your 2020 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 with the 5.3L V8 engine requires full synthetic SAE 0W-20 motor oil.

Oil Capacity: 5.8 quarts (5.5 liters) with filter change
